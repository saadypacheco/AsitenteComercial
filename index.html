SALES LEADERSHIP · DIGITAL ASSISTANT

Know what to do today — and run your whole sales team from one place.

An assistant for sales leaders managing large teams. It turns the WhatsApp chaos into a prioritized to-do list, and lets you act in one tap. Simple, from the phone.

Clickable prototype · interface shown in Spanish (fully multi-language) · sample data

The problem
The leader is drowning.
Managing ~1,300 agents by hand and by memory doesn't scale.
📱

Too much WhatsApp

Everything comes through one channel and important things slip through.

🚪

Who's leaving?

No early signal on agents about to quit or who needs help first.

🐢

Slow onboarding

New agents take long to produce, with no view of where they get stuck.

🧠

Run from memory

Follow-ups and decisions live in the leader's head → stress, no scale.

The solution
Not a CRM. An operating system for leaders.
Built on three pillars — and a smart WhatsApp inbox at the core.
🛡️

Control

See the whole team's status at a glance with a single Agent Score (0–100) per person.

🔭

Follow-up

Turn data into actions: who to call, who to help, who's about to leave — prioritized by urgency.

🚀

Development

Game-like onboarding that guides every new agent toward their first sale.

💬

Smart WhatsApp inbox

Capture every incoming message, triage by urgency, and reply from one place. Each message also feeds the system.

⚡

Act in one tap

Reply, call, assign a task, recognize a win — like a digital wallet. Everything is auto-recorded.

🎯

One number per agent

The Agent Score sums up Activity, Attendance, Compliance and Interaction — so you don't analyze 1,300 people.

How it works
A closed loop.
Every message and every action feeds the system back.
1

Signals in

WhatsApp messages, app activity, Zoom attendance, production.

2

Engine calculates

The Agent Score and prioritized alerts — automatically.

3

Leader acts

"What to do today" resolved in one or two taps.

4

Recorded

Everything is logged — and feeds back into the system.
